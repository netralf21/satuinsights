Market Research

Understanding markets, people, and context.

We design and deliver qualitative and quantitative research that reflects real-world perspectives and informs meaningful decisions. Our work is grounded in careful framing, appropriate methodology, and clear interpretation. Findings are relevant, credible, and actionable.

What This Includes

Qualitative research

In-depth interviews, focus groups, shop-alongs, ethnographic approaches, online communities, and expert sessions including C-suite and KOL interviews.

Quantitative research

Survey design, fieldwork management, data analysis, cross-tabulations, charts, and full reporting.

Full-cycle project management

From study design and recruitment through fieldwork, quality control, analysis, and final presentation.

Typical Engagements Include

  • Consumer and stakeholder research
  • Brand and positioning studies
  • Product and concept testing
  • Market opportunity and entry assessments
  • Ongoing tracking studies

Strategy and Advisory

Turning insight into direction.

Research has the greatest impact when it informs action. We work closely with clients to translate findings into clear strategic direction, supporting decisions around products, services, brands, and market entry.

Our advisory work is collaborative, pragmatic, and shaped by context. We help organisations move forward with confidence, not just understanding.

Support Areas Include

  • Insight-led strategy development
  • Product and service strategy
  • Brand and positioning refinement
  • Stakeholder sense-making

Research Support

Extending your team with trusted, senior support.

For agencies and organisations who need flexible, experienced research assistance, we provide support that integrates cleanly with existing teams and workflows.

This is particularly valuable for global or regional agencies who need a reliable local partner in Indonesia or Southeast Asia, and for internal teams who need senior research capacity without adding headcount.

Support Includes

  • Local representation and client management
  • Qualitative moderation and facilitation
  • Recruitment of hard-to-reach respondents
  • Report writing and analysis
  • Proposal development
  • Desk research and secondary research

Who We Work With

Partner agencies, regional and global. Corporates, SMEs, and start-ups. Founders validating a product or market. Teams who have data but need help making sense of it.
